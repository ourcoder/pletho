Advertiser Help - (Print)

Topics: Advanced Search, Search Results, Buying, Video Advert Url, Audio Advert Url, Image Advert Url.

The Advertiser Page

1. As a new advertiser on the Pletho platform it is best to start on the Advertiser page. This is the advertiser homepage, where you can search for advert listings posted by Pletho’s publishers.

2. At the top of the page (below the menu) you have a search bar; in this bar you can type any search text or keyword that you think would relate to the advert you are looking for; for example: “#TV #Manchester”.

3. To submit your search, just click the button labelled ‘Search’ on the right (displayed as an arrow on smartphones) or press enter on your keyboard.

4. The ‘Show All’ button displays all adverts currently available to purchase on the Pletho Marketplace.

5. The button marked ‘Advanced’ will display a new panel where Advertisers can search for campaigns that fit a very specific criteria.

The Advanced Search Panel

1. The first advanced search setting available is the locality. Here you can choose to restrict the results to show only Local or National adverts.

2. The Budget bar allows you to set minimum and maximum budget value, restricting the resulting adverts to only those that fall between these values.

3. The Lead bar also features a minimum and maximum value choice. This will restrict returning results to those that fall within the specific hours chosen.

4. There are four drop down lists that appear in the middle of the advanced search panel. These are; Country, City, Campaign Length and Publisher. These drop down lists feature only the countries, cities, campaign lengths and publishers that have adverts for sale right now. Choosing an option in any of these drop down list will restrict the results to that chosen option.

5. The last option on the advanced search panel is the advert types; Billboard, Bus, Cinema, Magazine, Newspaper, Radio, TV and Taxi. These are also populated from adverts that are currently for sale on the Pletho platform. You can choose one or many options here to restrict your search results to only those that are of this advert type.

6. The advanced search panel allows users to save advanced search criteria to load back at a later date. First choose the search criteria you would like to remember and then hit ‘Save’. If you then ‘Reset’ the advanced search panel and hit ‘Load’, Pletho will automatically load the previously saved advanced search options you provided earlier.

Search Results

1. From our search; “#Manchester #TV” we have returned three matching results. The result marked as ‘1’ in the image is in a detailed view, this was achieved by clicking on the advert once.

2. The advert marked as ‘2’ is in minimal view. All adverts return in this view mode by default, you will only see detailed information when you click on an advert.

3. Advert ‘3’ has been clicked twice. This has put the advert in the highlighted state. When an advert is in this state the two buttons; ‘Buy’ and ‘Share’ become active. If this advert is clicked again it will return back to its minimal view (as seen in ‘2’).

4. As we can see from this example, three of a maximum of twelve adverts are being displayed. Nine adverts did not match our search criteria and therefor were not displayed in the results.

5. When an advert has been highlighted (advert ‘3’), the ‘Share’ button becomes active. This button allows you to share information that pertains to this specific advert. Several sharing options are available, including popular social networks and email; allowing you to share adverts directly with colleagues, friends or family in just a couple of clicks.

6. The ‘Buy’ button works the same as the ‘Share’ button; in that it only becomes active when an advert has been highlighted. Only one advert can be highlighted at any one time.

Buying / Purchasing / Ordering

1. After you have clicked the ‘Buy’ button you will be greeted with a small panel to input specific details about your campaign. The first is the ‘Preferred Date’ that you would like the advert to air. To choose a date simply tap where it says ‘Tap to pick’ and a calendar will appear. Please note: at the top of the page information regarding the earliest date and time the campaign can be ordered for will appear. Ordering before this date is not be possible.

2. The ‘Preferred Time’ option will only appear for specific advert types that require it. To choose a time simply click the text “Tap to choose” and then pick the time that best fits your campaign.

3. Please enter the amount of ‘Flexibility’ you would like your campaign to have. This will help the Publisher when they set an exact date and time that your advert will air. You can set the ‘Flexibility’ as minutes, hours, days, weeks, none or open (if you are unsure).

4. The ‘Advert Url’ field requires an internet address that points to the advert you want the Publisher to display.
For example;

A TV advert would be a video, so would be hosted on Vimeo and have an internet address similar to: http://vimeo.com/14504562

A Radio advert would be sound, so would be hosted on Soundcloud and have an internet address similar to: http://soundcloud.com/s/radio

A Magazine advert would be an image, so would be hosted on Imgur and have an internet address similar to: http://i.imgur.com/tiC9ufb.jpg

Using Imgur you can even share an album of images with the Publisher.

If you require more help getting your advert online, please scroll down to see the in-depth tutorials.

5. The default currency that loads with the advert is the one that the Publisher will require payment in. If you wish to see what the cost is in your own currency you can do so by using the currency drop down list. Please note that your bank may charge transaction and conversion fees when purchasing in foreign currencies.

6. Once you have entered all the details into the fields provided you can now click the ‘Buy’ text to forward your order on to the Publisher for approval. At any time during this process you can click the ‘X’ (cross) icon in the top right corner to exit and close the purchase window.

Campaigns Help - (Print)

Topics: Pending, Queued, Live, Disputes, Completed.

Pending Campaigns

Pending campaigns can only be progressed to the next stage by Publishers. The Publishers will be able to see all the details of the campaign including the preferred date and time that the Advertiser would like their campaign to air. During the approval process the Publisher can change this date to a more accurate time and date if the preferred date or time provided by the Advertiser is not available.

Pending campaigns will also include denied campaigns for Advertisers. If a campaign gets refused by a Publisher, a denied campaign will be sent back to the Advertiser with a reason as to why this occured.

Advertiser Interaction

1. To interact with your campaign, click it to highlight it so that the action buttons become active.

2. The 'Remove' button can be used if you need to cancel a campaign. When you click 'Remove' a new window will appear asking you to state a reason why you are removing the campaign. Please enter the reason and then click 'Submit' to complete the cancellation. For campaigns denied by the Publisher the remove button will remove this campaign from your Pending Campaigns page.

3. The 'Help' button can be used to contact Pletho's support team about a specific campaign. After clicking this button a new window will appear. Inside the textbox provided please detail what your problem is and then click 'Submit'. A member of Pletho's support team will respond to the email address provided when you signed up.

Publisher Interaction

1. To interact with the campaign, click it to highlight it so that the action buttons become active.

2. The 'Approve' button will progress the campaign to the next stage. After you click this button you will be asked to provide a more accurate date and time for when the Advertiser's advert will air. Please be as accurate as possible as the Advertiser will see this date.

3. The 'Remove' button can be used if you need to cancel a campaign. When you click 'Remove' a new window will appear asking you to state a reason why you are removing the campaign. Please enter the reason and then click 'Submit' to complete the cancellation. The Advertiser will get to see the reason why their campaign was removed, please advise them on how they can avoid this in the future.

4. The 'Help' button can be used to contact Pletho's support team about a specific campaign. After clicking this button a new window will appear. Inside the textbox provided please detail what your problem is and then click 'Submit'. A member of Pletho's support team will respond to the email address provided when you signed up.


Queued Campaigns

Queued campaigns can only be progressed to the next stage by Advertisers. The Advertiser will be able to see all the details of the campaign; including the newly added actual date and time that the Publisher provided for when the campaign will air. The Advertiser can choose to pay for this campaign now and progress it to the next stage, or remove it entirely from Pletho, cancelling the campaign.

Campaigns that are not paid for in time will be automatically cancelled.

Advertiser Interaction

1. To interact with the campaign, click it to highlight it so that the action buttons become active.

2. The 'Pay' button will progress the campaign to the next stage. After clicking the 'Pay' button Pletho will contact Stripe, our payment platform. You will be asked for a card number, expiry date and the CVC. You also have the option to allow Stripe to remember your card details by providing the system with a mobile phone number. After you have entered the payment details, click the blue 'Pay' button provided at the bottom to complete the transaction. Pletho and Stripe communicate safely using secure protocols, your data is extremely important to us and we take security very seriously.

3. The 'Remove' button can be used if you need to cancel a campaign. When you click 'Remove' a new window will appear asking you to state a reason why you are removing the campaign. Please enter the reason and then click 'Submit' to complete the cancellation.

4. The 'Help' button can be used to contact Pletho's support team about a specific campaign. After clicking this button a new window will appear. Inside the textbox provided please detail what your problem is and then click 'Submit'. A member of Pletho's support team will respond to the email address provided when you signed up.

Publisher Interaction

1. To interact with the campaign, click it to highlight it so that the action buttons become active.

2. The 'Help' button can be used to contact Pletho's support team about a specific campaign. After clicking this button a new window will appear. Inside the textbox provided please detail what your problem is and then click 'Submit'. A member of Pletho's support team will respond to the email address provided when you signed up.


Live Campaigns

Live campaigns can be progressed by Advertisers and Publishers and they can either go on to the disputed section or become completed campaigns.

A Publisher only has the option to progress a campaign to a disputed state; a Publisher may do this when problems occur with the date or time an advert will air, entering into a dispute will allow the Publisher to communicate directly with the Advertiser to resolve the situation.

An Advertiser has the option to complete their campaign as well as dispute it. An Advertiser should only complete their campaign once they are happy that their campaign is complete and that their advert aired correctly. If an Advertiser has problems with how the advert aired or if the advert did not air they can then dispute the campaign and try to resolve the problem with Publisher or request a refund.

Advertiser Interaction

1. To interact with the campaign, click it to highlight it so that the action buttons become active.

2. The 'Complete' button should be used when your campaign has been aired successfully. After pressing the button the campaign will be moved to the completed section. Your campaign is now complete, congratulations.

3. The 'Dispute' button should be used when your campaign aired incorrectly, or if it didn't air at all. Disputing a campaign will allow you to communicate with the Publisher to request a refund or to have the advert rescheduled for another air date. Please give a reason why you are disputing the campaign in the box provided and how you would like the situation to be resolved. Once this is complete, click 'Submit' to finalise the dispute submission.

4. The 'Share' button can be used to share details of your campaign with colleagues, friends and family. After clicking the button a new box will appear allowing you to share the campaign details via select social networks or by email.

5. The 'Help' button can be used to contact Pletho's support team about a specific campaign. After clicking this button a new window will appear. Inside the textbox provided please detail what your problem is and then click 'Submit'. A member of Pletho's support team will respond to the email address provided when you signed up.

Publisher Interaction

1. To interact with the campaign, click it to highlight it so that the action buttons become active.

2. The 'Dispute' button should be used when the campaign cannot be aired on time or if there is another problem. Please give a reason why the campaign cannot air on time and when it can be rescheduled for, then click 'Submit' to complete the dispute submission. If the advert cannot be aired due to unforeseen circumstances, please refund the Advertiser and mention this in the reason box before submitting.

3. The 'Share' button can be used to share details of the campaign with colleagues, friends and family. After clicking the button a new box will appear allowing you to share the campaign details via select social networks or by email.

A member of Pletho's support team will respond to the email address provided when you signed up.


Disputed Campaigns

Disputed campaigns can only be progressed to the next stage by Advertisers. While the campaign is being disputed both the Advertiser and Publisher are able to use the comments window to communicate with each other. When the Advertiser is happy with the outcome of a dispute they can then proceed the campaign to its final completed state. A dispute resolution may come in the form of a refund or the advert airing at a different date and time if it missed its original air date.

Please note: Pletho support staff can see all disputes and are able to join a dispute conversation when necessary.

After clicking the 'Close' button a new box will appear asking you for the reason why you are closing the dispute and whether you are happy with the solution provided by the Publisher. Once you have entered this information click 'Submit' to complete the campaign.

Inside the textbox provided please detail what your problem is and then click 'Submit'. A member of Pletho's support team will respond to the email address provided when you signed up.

Please communicate with the Advertiser about the problems they are having with their campaign and how they would like the situation to be resolved. Publisher Help - (Print)

Topics: Registering, Adding Adverts, Modifying Adverts, Airing Adverts.

Registering

Before you can start publishing your adverts for sale on the Pletho marketplace you will need to register. During the registration process a member of the Pletho Team may contact you via email or telephone as part of the Publisher Approval Process.

Publisher Approval Process

1. To begin the Publisher Approval Process please click the button named 'Start'.

2. You can enter the details required manually or populate them by connecting to your LinkedIn account. Once you have completed the form press the 'Submit' button to send the application to Pletho for approval. We aim to reply within 72 hours.

3. Once you have been approved by Pletho you can now add payment details to your Publisher account. This is where all payment proceeds from advert sales on the Pletho marketplace will be sent. Our payments system is supported by Stripe's Merchant platform; so for this step you will be forwarded to Stripe's merchant registration form. Please submit all the information that Stripe requires for your merchant account to be created and then authorise access for Pletho to use this information.

4. After you authorise Stripe to share your merchant account information with Pletho you will be sent back to Pletho. Please wait while Pletho adds the details provided by Stripe to our system. Once complete you will be forwarded back to the Publisher page so that you can begin to add your adverts to Pletho's marketplace.


Adding Adverts

Once a Publisher has been approved and shared their Stripe merchant account information with Pletho the 'Submit New Advertising Listing' page will become active in the Publisher section. From here you can submit adverts directly to Pletho's marketplace

Listing Guide

1. The first set of fields and options available for your advert listing are required entries. These include; the 'Area' that the advert is being display in, please choose 'Local' or 'National', a 'Price' for the campaign that the Advertiser will pay, the main 'City' that the advert is airing in, an 'Ad Type' for the campaign, please choose from the list, the 'Lead Time' that you require to process the campaign, any 'Tags' or keywords that you wish to be associated with your advert, the average 'Reach' amount expected for this advert and the 'Campaign Length' that this advert will have.

2. The next part of the listing page is the advert 'Description'. This is a required field, please enter as much information as you can about your advert here.

3. The next section of the advert listing is populated automatically from your Stripe merchant account. This includes; the 'Publisher' name/identity, the 'Currency' you receive payments in, the 'Country' that the advert is based in and the 'Quantity' of stock available for this advert. You can change the 'Currency' and 'Country' manually. However, please be aware that foriegn currency payments may incur fees and charges levied by your bank or Stripe. To change your advert from having 'Unlimited' stock, please untick the option box and enter the amount of stock available in 'Quantity' field.

4. Now you can add a 'Logo' for your advert. This can be a logo that relates to the Publisher Company or one that is specific for this advert. Please note; the background colour for your logo will be generated from the pixels at the center of the image.

5. The last section of the advertising listing page are optional fields. These includes; the 'Reach in TSA', the 'Average Hours', an 'ABC1 Profile', the 'Gender Ratio' and the 'Age Demographic'. If you are entering data into these fields it is advised that you enter a 'Source' for this information in the field provided.

6. Now that your listing is complete, please click the 'Publish' button to continue.

7. You will see a preview of your advert before it goes live, please double check that all the information visible is correct. This is how your advert will look to Advertisers viewing the Pletho marketplace on a smartphone. It may be necessary to change/tweak certain aspects about your advert to tailor the output for these smaller display devices. Once you are happy with how your advert looks, click the 'Confirm' button to finish.

8. Once the advert has been added to the marketplace, Pletho will automatically forward you to the listing on the Advertiser page. From here you can highlight your advert and 'Share' its details with selected social networks or via email.


Modifying Adverts

Adverts published onto the Pletho marketplace can still be changed or updated while live. A Publisher can do this by searching for their advert in the 'Advertiser' section. The easiest way to do this would be by searching for your Publisher name/identity (you can find this on the 'Publisher' page).

Advert Management

1. To interact with the advert, click it to highlight it so that the action buttons become active.

2. If the advert you highlighted is one that you own, the button named 'Modify' will appear.

3. You also have the option to 'Share' this advert to a range of social networks or by email.

4. Once you click the 'Modify' button a new window will appear. In this window you can control the stock 'Quantity', the adverts 'Price' and the 'Lead Time' that you require.

5. You can also 'Delete' the advert from the Pletho marketplace by using the button provided.

6. Once you have made the modifications required, click the 'Update' button to confirm the changes.

Once you are on this page, locate the button shown above. It will usually be found below the sounds playback view, click it and then save the file to your computer.
